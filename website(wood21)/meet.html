MEET THE ANIMALS

We rescue cows, pigs, chickens, ducks, rabbits, goats (and more!) and provide them lifelong care and
sanctuary. Here at Woodstock Sanctuary they are provided proper care and shelter and are able to stay
with their families, living peaceful lives full of love and joy.
